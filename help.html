Help

Uploading A Photo

1. Click the choose file button under Upload your Image Below

2. Select the image you want from your devices file system

3. To record your audio caption, allow the webpage to access your microphone then click start recording.

4. Click stop recording when you are finished, and play back the audio by pressing play on the media player.

5. Pressing record again will overwrite the previous recording. When you are satisfied with your audio, select the three dots on the media player to download it

6. Now that your audio file is downloaded, upload the file by pressing choose file under Upload your audio. Note that if you already have an audio file you want to use steps 3-5 can be skipped

7. Once both an image and audio file are uploaded, select the the desired destination album from the dropdown list.

8. You are now ready to submit! Click the submit button at the bottom of the page to add your image and audio to the selected album.

Viewing Photos in My Gallery

To view all the images you have uploaded simple navigate to the My Gallery page on the top navigation bar. Click on any image to enlarge it, and close by clicking the X in the top right.

Adding a photo to any album automatically uploads it to My Gallery.
